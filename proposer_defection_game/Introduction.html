Welcome to the Game

You are in a group of six participants. In each round:

  • One participant will be randomly assigned as the Proposer.
  • The proposer decides how to split €{{ C.TOTAL_AMOUNT }} among all six participants (including themselves).
  • The other five will either accept or reject the proposed allocation.
  • If the majority accepts, the proposer can then defect and make a new allocation.
  • This new allocation is the one that will be implemented
  • This process repeats for three rounds.
  • If majority rejects, everyone gets 0€ that round
  • Your total payoff is the sum of what you receive for the three rounds
